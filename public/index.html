1 LOCOMOTION IDEA

Our first idea was to create a locomotion technique that is controlled with the position of your arms : With the two arms in front of you at the same height, you go forward. If the right hand is higher, you go left and vice versa. If your two arms are behind you, you go backward and if your two arms are in a rest position then you don’t move.
